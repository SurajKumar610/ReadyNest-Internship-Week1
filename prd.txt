Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project: Dataset Analysis & Reporting Platform

Version: 1.0
Domain: Data Analytics
Target Users: Analysts, Business Teams, Students, Researchers
Objective: Build a complete end-to-end dataset analysis and reporting system that loads raw data, cleans it, performs statistical and exploratory analysis, generates insights, and presents results through interactive dashboards and automated reports.

1. Project Overview

The Dataset Analysis & Reporting platform will enable users to:

Import datasets from multiple sources.
Clean and preprocess raw data.
Perform statistical and exploratory data analysis.
Discover relationships and patterns.
Create visualizations and dashboards.
Generate business insights automatically.
Perform forecasting and predictive modeling.
Conduct A/B testing and advanced reporting.

The system should support CSV, Excel, SQL databases, and APIs.

2. Goals
Primary Goals
Simplify dataset analysis workflow.
Automate reporting and insight generation.
Provide interactive dashboards.
Support predictive analytics and forecasting.
Success Metrics
Support datasets up to 1 million rows.
Dashboard response time < 2 seconds.
Generate reports automatically.
Produce at least 5–10 meaningful insights from a dataset.
